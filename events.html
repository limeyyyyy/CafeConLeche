The Venue

Cafe Con Leche is part of SODO's premier live music venue, Club Sur. The elegant space offers top quality sound, lighting and a large dance floor where you can enjoy your favorite bands or host a private party. Join us for weekly dances or check our calendar to see what exciting global act is visiting us soon.

Cuban Dinner, Dessert, Dance Lesson and Salsa Dancing

Once a month, Cafe con Leche offers the opportunity for you to come and enjoy a fantastic Cuban experience. Guests are seated for dinner at 8 PM (or 9:00, depending on the event), after which they sweep onto the dance floor for a salsa lesson led by experience hoofers. Once they get the hang of the steps, dancers are free to wile away the night by moving to the music of a Latin/Salsa band. Reservations required for dinner.
